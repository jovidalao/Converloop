Integrated Dialogue Loop

The loop and dialogue idea are merged into one symbol. No separate chat bubble is placed inside the icon.

1. Speech Loop Tail

The lower return arrow includes a small speech-tail gesture, so the loop itself becomes a conversation bubble outline.

Recommended: strongest merge of loop + dialogue while staying compact.

2. Bubble Outline Loop

The full loop is a rounded speech-bubble outline with arrow endpoints. It is conceptually clear, but has more turns and can feel busier.

Tradeoff: clearer dialogue shape, slightly less clean as an app icon.

3. Chat Flow Arrows

Two message lines become the arrows. It is very simple and technical, but less recognizably a speech bubble.

Tradeoff: cleanest geometry, weaker dialogue metaphor.

Speech Loop Tail is the best next direction. It keeps the simple loop silhouette, but the lower arrow also behaves like the tail of a dialogue bubble. That feels integrated instead of assembled.
