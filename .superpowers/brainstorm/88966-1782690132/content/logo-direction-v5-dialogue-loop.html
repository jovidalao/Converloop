Dialogue Loop Variants

Three ways to connect the loop with conversation while keeping the clean tech-blue direction.

1. Bubble Core

The center point becomes a compact chat bubble. The loop still carries the brand idea, while the middle clearly says conversation.

Recommended: explicit enough, still clean at app-icon scale.

2. Reply Pair

Two small bubbles sit inside the loop, so it reads as back-and-forth conversation. Stronger concept, but busier.

Tradeoff: clearer at large sizes, more fragile at tiny sizes.

3. Ellipsis Core

Minimal conversation cue: the center point becomes typing dots. It stays very clean, but feels less distinctive than a speech bubble.

Tradeoff: best for simplicity, weakest for brand meaning.

My read

Use Bubble Core if you want the loop to connect to conversation without losing the clean tech-blue identity. It is the strongest balance of app-icon clarity and product meaning.
